Kazuha Best Build (Support)

The best build for Kazuha is Support. This build consists of increasing Kazuha's Elemental Proficiency so that he deals more damage in Whirlwind reactions. This build will use the Oath of Freedom weapon and the Green Shadow artifact set.



Kazuha Best Weapon

The best one for Kazuha is the Oath of Freedom. This weapon will significantly increase Kazuha's Elemental Proficiency, as well as increase his team's damage through its passive. Another option would be Xipho's Moonlight, however, it is inferior as it is a 4-star weapon.



Better

Pledge for Freedom

Elemental Proficiency

A part of the "Millenary Concert" that floats in the wind. Increases Damage dealt by 10-20%. When activating Elemental Reaction, the character will obtain a Resistance Token, which can be activated in 0.5s, even when the character is not on the battlefield. Having 2 Symbols of Resistance consumes them all, and makes all characters on the team obtain the effect of "Millennial Concert: Song of Resistance" for 12s: Increases Damage of Normal Attack, Charged Attack, Immersive Attack by 16-32 %, however ATK will be increased by 20-40%. Within 20s after activation, it is not possible to obtain Resistance Tokens again. Among all effects of multiple values, effects of the same value cannot be accumulated.

Other Weapon options for Kazuha are: Iron Thorn, Reforged Mist Cutter and Amenoma Kageuta Blade.



Kazuha Best Artifact Set

The best artifact set for Kazuha is the Green Shadow one. This set will increase Anemo Damage Bonus by 15%, Whirlwind damage by 60%, and decrease enemie's elemental resistance by 40% depending on the infused element type.

Kazuha Artifacts Sub-Attributes

The sub-attributes in this build will be Elemental Proficiency and Energy Recharge. Critical Damage and Critical Rate will not be necessary as the Whirlwind reaction is not affected by them, therefore, such reaction must be scaled through Elemental Proficiency and character level. The higher the character's level, the greater the Whirlwind's damage.


Kazuha Talent Priority

The first talent you should raise is Ultimate, as it is the talent that causes the most damage. Elemental Skill will be second and Normal Attack will be third. Generally speaking, Kazuha doesn't need to prioritize his talents, as most of his damage will come from the reactions he causes.
