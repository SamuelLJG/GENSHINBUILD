Furina Best Build (Sub-DPS)

The best build for the Furina is Sub-DPS. In this Build Furina will use your ability to cause impressive damage off the field and use your Supreme to offer considerable buffs to the team, allowing it to be the centerpiece of a wide variety of team compositions.



Furina Best weapon

The best for Furina is Splendor of Tranquil Waters. This is Furina's signature weapon and the best option for her. It increases all stats relevant to Furina's performance. There's not much to say other than the raw stats given specifically to Furina.

The Fleuve Cendre Ferryman can be another good option for the character if you don't have her signature weapon.



Best

Splendor of Tranquil Waters

CRIT DMG %

When the equipping character's current HP increases or decreases, Elemental Skill DMG dealt will be increased by 8-16% for 6s. Max 3 stacks. This effect can be triggered once every 0.2s. When other party members' current HP increases or decreases, the equipping character's Max HP will be increased by 14-24% for 6s. Max 2 stacks. This effect can be triggered once every 0.2s. The aforementioned effects can be triggered even if the wielder is off-field.


Optional

Fleuve Cendre Ferryman

Energy Recharge

Increases Elemental Skill CRIT Rate by 8-16%. Additionally, increases Energy Recharge by 16-32% for 5s after using an Elemental Skill.


Other weapon options for Furina are: Primordial Jade Cutter, Wolf-Fang and Key of Khaj-Nisut.



Furina Best Artifact Set

The best set of artifacts for Furina is the Golden Troupe. This set of artifacts is the most recommended for Furina, as it increases the damage of the character's ability while she is out of the field, which is perfect for Furina who will have the Off-Field DPS function.

Furina Artifacts Sub-Attributes

As you will need to deal as much damage as possible, it is important to build CRIT DMG and CRIT Rate. You can also build HP%, ATK%, Elemental Proficiency and Energy Recharge, but they are second priority.


Furina Talent Priority

Furina's Talent that you should prioritize is the Elemental Skill, as it is the Skill she will use most on the field. The Elemental Burst is second priority and the Normal Attack is not necessary.
