Freminet Best Build (Main DPS)

The best build for Freminet is Main DPS. This entire build revolves around increasing Freminet's Physical Damage, making her skills deal the greatest amount of Physical Damage possible, exerting the character's full potential.



Freminet Best Weapon

The best weapon for the Freminet is the Song of the Pines. This sword's main attribute is Physical Damage, which greatly benefits Freminet. This weapon also has a passive that increases ATK damage and Normal Attack Speed.



Better

Song of the Pines

Physical Damage Bonus

A part of the Millennial Concert that floats in the wind. Increases ATK by 16-32%. When the Normal Attack or Charged Attack hits enemies, the character will obtain a Symbol of Sighs, which can be activated once every 0.3s. Having 4 Whisper Symbols consumes them all, and makes all characters on the team obtain the effect of "Millennial Concert: Song of the Raising of the Flag" for 12s: Speed. Normal Attack will be increased by 12-24%, however ATK will be increased by 20-40%. Within 20s after activation, it is not possible to obtain Whisper Tokens again. Among all effects of multiple values, effects of the same value cannot be accumulated.


Optional

Serpent's Backbone

Critical Rate

When the player is in combat, every 4s increases damage dealt by 6-10% and 3-2% of Damage received. This effect can be stacked up to 5 times, not being reset after the character leaves the field, but will reduce 1 layer of the effect when the character receives Damage.


Other Weapon options for Freminet are: Silver Star Buried in the Snow (F2P), Tomb of the Wolf, Rough Sword, Archaic Prototype (F2P), Celestial Pride, Stonebreaker Red Horns and Luxurious Sea Lord.



Freminet Best Artifact Set

The best artifact set for Freminet is the Pale Flame. This set, among all, is the best for Physical Damage characters, this includes Freminet. Another option, however, less effective, would be two pieces from Pale Flame and two from Bloodstained Knight.

Freminet Artifacts Sub-Attributes

For artifact sub-attributes, the priorities are Critical Rate and Critical Damage. If possible, it would also be good to have ATK% and Energy Recharge, but the priorities remain the same.


Freminet Talent Priority

A large part of Freminet's damage comes from his Elemental Skill, so it must be leveled first, then the Normal Attack and then the Ultimate. All of Freminet's talents are important and none should be left unlevelled, they are all important for the character's kit.
