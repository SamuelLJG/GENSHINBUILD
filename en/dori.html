Dori Best Build (Support)

The best build for Dori is the Support build. This build simply consists of improving her healing capacity and her energy recharge, so that she can heal well and have a good recharge to use her Ultimate more constantly.



Dori Best Weapons

The best weapons for Dori are the Sword of Favonius and the Sword of Sacrifice, both weapons are very flexible and have a great amount of Energy Recharge as a base, which is great for Dori in this build as support.

Other Weapon options for Dori are: Celestial Pride, Katsuragi's Greatsword (F2P) and The Bell.



Dori Best Artifact Sets

The best artifact sets for Dori are the two Seal of Insulation pieces and two Millelith Firmes. With these two sets, Dori will provide a good amount of healing and will also be able to use her Ultimate more constantly due to the greater Energy Recharge she will have.


If you need a Buffer on your team, you can use the Ancient Royal Ritual set, using the Ultimate will increase the ATK of all team members by 20% for 12s.

Dori Artifacts Sub-Attributes

For artifact sub-attributes, the priorities are HP% and Energy Recharge. You can also have Critical Rate and Elemental Proficiency, but these attributes are second priority. It is recommended that Dori has at least 200% Energy Recharge.


Dori Talent Priority

Dori's Talent that you should prioritize is Ultimate, as it is the character's source of healing as a Support. Elemental Skill is second priority and Basic Attack is not required.

Dori Talents



Normal Attack: Fantastic Sword Dance


Normal Attack


Perform up to 3 consecutive strikes.


Charged Attack


Drains Stamina over time to perform a continuous spinning attack against all nearby enemies.
At the end of the sequence, he performs a powerful cut.


Immersive Attack


Dives mid-air to slam the ground, damaging enemies in its path and inflicting AoE damage on impact.


Lamp of Protection: Reliever Cannon


Aim a Protection Lamp at enemies to fire Soothing Shots, inflicting Electro Damage.
When Soothing Shots hit opponents, they create 2 Aftermarket Ammo that will automatically chase nearby opponents, inflicting Electro Damage.


Alcazarzaray's Meticulousness


Summons the Genie of the Lamp to offer all types of assistance to customers.


Genie of the Lamp


Connects with a nearby character. The connected character will:
• Continuously Regenerate Health based on Dori's Maximum Health;
• Continuously regenerate Elemental Energy.
• Become affected by Electro.

Additionally, when the connection between the Genie of the Lamp and the character touches enemies, it will inflict Electro Damage against them once every 0.4s.
Only one Genie of the Lamp can exist at a time.



An Eye for Gold


When a character connected to the Genie of the Lamp triggers an Electrically Charged, Superconducting, Overload, Acceleration, Intensification, Superbloom, Electro Swirl, or Electro Crystallization reaction, the Cooldown of Protection Lamp: Soothing Cannon is reduced by 1s.
This effect can trigger once every 3s.


Compound Interest


When Soothing Shots or Aftermarket Ammo from Lamp of Protection: Soothing Cannon hit opponents, Dori will recover 5 Elemental Energy for every 100% Energy Recharge possessed. With each use of Lamp of Protection: Soothing Cannon, you can only restore Elemental Energy once in this way, and at most you can restore 15 Elemental Energy.


Unexpected Sale


Has a 25% chance of returning some of the materials used when crafting Character and Weapon Materials.
