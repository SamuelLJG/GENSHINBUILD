Baizhu Best Build (Support)

The best build for Baizhu is Support. This build specializes in increasing Baizhu's healing based on its HP, using the Indigo Radiance catalyst and the Sea Dyed Shell artifact set as a weapon to make this possible.



Baizhu Best Weapon

The best weapon for Baizhu is Indigo Radiance. This is Baizhu's signature weapon. It improves energy recharge and increases the character's elemental proficiency based on their Max health. Another option would be the Amber Prototype.

The "Gift of Primordial Jade" effect can also be triggered when the character who has this weapon equipped is on your team, but off the battlefield.

Baizhu Best Artifact Set

The best artifact set for the Baizhu is the Sea-Dyed Shell. This set, in addition to healing, can cause damage to enemies based on the healing done. Another set option would be Memories of the Forest.

Baizhu Artifacts Sub-Attributes

For artifact sub-attributes, the priorities are HP%, Energy Recharge, and Elemental Proficiency. In order for Baizhu to maximize the damage of his passive's elemental reactions, it is recommended that he has between 40,000 and 50,000 Maximum Life in total.


Baizhu Talent Priority

As Support, the Baizhu talents you should level up first are Elemental Skill and Ultimate. Both are important for the character to be able to perform his role perfectly as a Support. Normal Attack will only be necessary if you want to use Baizhu as an activator of dendro reactions.



Baizhu Best Teams


As Baizhu is a Support that increases the damage of all reactions involving dendro, he can be paired with any character that benefits from dendro reactions. However, teams that involve Super Bloom and Haste reactions are considered the best for Baizhu.

Baizhu Talents



Normal Attack: Golden Acupuncture


Normal Attack


Performs up to 4 attacks that inflict Dendro Damage.


Charged Attack


Consumes a certain amount of Stamina to inflict AoE Dendro Damage after a short period of channeling.


Immersive Attack


Gathering the power of the Dendro, it dives towards the ground in mid-air, inflicting damage on all enemies in its path. Inflicts AoE Dendro Damage upon impact with the ground.


Universal Diagnosis


Controls a Serum Spirit, causing it to attack nearby enemies, dealing Dendro Damage.
After making up to 3 attacks, or when there are no enemies nearby, the Seric Spirit will return, regenerating Life for all nearby team characters; the amount of Health regenerated is based on Baizhu's Max Health.


Holistic Healing


Enter the Pulsating Edict state, creating an Impenetrable Shield that has a 250% Dendro Damage absorption effect.
While in the Pulsating Edict state, it will generate a new Impenetrable Shield every 2.5s.

Under the circumstances below, it will regenerate Life for the active characters of your team on the battlefield, the amount regenerated is based on Baizhu's Maximum Life, and it will attack enemies by launching Spiriveio, causing Dendro Damage: • When a character is protected by the Shield Impenetrable and a new Impenetrable Shield is generated;
• When the effect of the Impenetrable Shield ends or when it is destroyed.



Five Eternal Fortunes


Baizhu gains different effects depending on the active character's current Health:
• When Health is less than 50%, Baizhu gains 20% Healing Bonus.
• When Health is equal to or greater than 50%, Baizhu gains 25% Dendro Damage Bonus.


Mother earth


Characters healed by Impenetrable Shield will gain the "Age of Verdant Protection" effect: Every 1,000 points of Baizhu's Max Health below 50,000 increases the Damage of Burn, Bloom, Super Bloom, and Germination reactions by 2%. The Damage of Intensify and Propagation reactions increases by 0.8%. The duration is 6s.


Herbal Nutrition


When Baizhu is on the team, interacting with certain collectibles heals the team for 2.5% of Baizhu's Max Health.
