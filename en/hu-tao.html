Hu Tao Best Build (Main DPS)

The best build for Hu Tao is Main DPS. The objective of this build is to increase the damage of Vaporize and Fusion reactions using the Crimson Flame Witch set. This build will also improve Hu Tao's personal Pyro damage.



Hu Tao Best Weapon

The best weapon for Hu Tao is the Homa Staff, as it increases HP and ATK based on Hu Tao's maximum health and also the amount of Critical Damage, which gives you more freedom to focus on critical rate on artifacts.


Another weapon option is the Crimson Sands Staff, which provides additional ATK based on Elemental Proficiency, increases ATK when hitting an enemy with Elemental Skill, and provides Critical Rate as its main attribute.

Other Weapon options for Hu Tao are: White Tassel (F2P), Duel Spear, Dragon's Bane, Primeval Jade Spear.



Hu Tao Best Artifact Set

The best artifact set for Hu Tao is The Witch of the Crimson Flames. The two pieces grant Bonus Pyro Damage and the four pieces increase the Damage of Vaporization and Fusion elemental reactions, which is the main objective of this build.


Another set of artifacts that you can use is the Nostalgic Reminiscence, which increases the Damage of Normal and Charged Attacks, however, it makes it difficult to use the Ultimate due to the loss of elemental energy after using the elemental skill.

Hu Tao Artifacts Sub-Attributes

For artifact sub-attributes, the priorities are Critical Rate and Critical Damage. Secondly, Elemental Proficiency and HP %, it is recommended to have between 100 to 250 Proficiency and 30000 Maximum Life for Hu Tao. Lastly ATK % and HP Flat.


Hu Tao Talent Priority

The Hu Tao talents you should level up first are Normal Attack and Elemental Skill, as the two are the biggest source of Damage in Hu Tao's kit. The Supreme must also be elevated, however, it is a second priority.




Hu Tao Best Teams


The best team for Hu Tao can be formed by Vaporization and Fusion. They are both good and will have good synergy with Hu Tao. Then you can choose your best team with the characters you have.

This team will have Yelan and Xingqiu, who will use their Ultimates to provide Hydro Damage to Hu Tao 's Normal Attacks, causing her to trigger several vaporization reactions and Zhongli as Support, providing Shield and ATK Buff.

Charged Attack


Consumes a certain amount of Stamina to launch yourself forward, inflicting Damage along the way.


Immersive Attack

Beyond Guide


Only eternal flames can cleanse this world. Hu Tao consumes a portion of Health and repels nearby enemies, entering the Butterfly Dance state.


Butterfly Dance


• Butterfly Dance increases Hu Tao's ATK based on his Max Health. The ATK obtained in this way cannot exceed 400% of your Basic ATK;
• ATK Damage is transformed into Pyro Damage. This elemental damage cannot be replaced by another elemental enchantment;
• The Charged Attack can cause enemies hit by the Bloody Petals effect;
• Increases Hu Tao's resistance to interruptions.


Bloody Petals


Enemies affected by Bloody Petals will receive Pyro Damage once every 4s. This damage is considered Elemental Skill damage. There can only be one Blood Petals effect per target, and its duration can only be renewed by Hu Tao.

Butterfly Dance will only dissipate when its duration ends or when Hu Tao is removed from combat or defeated.


Peacemaker of Spirits


Swings your flaming soul, dealing large AoE Pyro DMG.
When hitting enemies, it restores Hu Tao's Health based on his Maximum Health. This effect can be applied to up to 5 enemies hit.
On hit, if Hu Tao's Health is less than or equal to 50%, it can cause greater Damage and Healing.



Chrysalis


After the Butterfly Dance status applied by Guide from Beyond ends, the CRIT Rate of all characters (minus Hu Tao) in the team increases by 12% for 8s.


Boiling Blood


When Hu Tao's Health is less than or equal to 50%, he will obtain 33% bonus Pyro Damage.


The More, the Better


When a Perfect Cooking is made, there is an 18% chance of receiving a Weird Cooking of the same genre.
