Diona Talents



Normal Attack: Hunter's Marksmanship


Normal Attack


Perform up to 5 consecutive shots with the bow.


Charged Attack


Performs a more accurate Aimed Shot attack with more damage. While aiming, ice will accumulate on the arrow. A fully charged arrow will deal Cryo DMG.


Immersive Attack


Fires a hail of arrows into the air before falling and impacting the ground, dealing AoE Damage on impact.


Icy Cat's Claw


Fires an Icy Cat Claw that deals Cryo DMG and creates a shield. The shield's Damage Absorption is based on Diona's Max Health, and its duration depends on the number of claws that hit their target.


Touch


Quickly fire 2 Icy Cat Claws.


Hold


Quickly move back and fire 5 Icy Cat Claws. The shield created by the Hold attack will gain a 75% Damage Absorption bonus. The shield has a 250% Cryo Damage
Absorption bonus, and when formed applies the Cryo Element to your own character for a short duration.


Killer Cocktail


Throws a special iced cocktail that deals Cryo DMG for an AoE and creates a Drunken Mist for an AoE.


Drunken Mist


Deals continuous Cryo DMG to enemies within AoE.
Continuously regenerates character's Health within AoE.



Cat's Tail Secret Menu


Characters protected by Icy Cat's Claw have its Speed. Move. increased by 10% and your Stamina Consumption decreased by 10%.


Drunk Fun


Enemies that enter Killer Cocktail's AoE deal 10% less ATK for 15s.


Free Appetizers


When Perfect Cooking is done on a food with a recovery effect, there is a 12% chance of receiving double production.
