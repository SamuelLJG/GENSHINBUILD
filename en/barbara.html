Barbara Best Build (Support)

The best build for Barbara is Support. This build specializes in Pure Healing, which means you have no problems keeping your team alive. Extraordinary Stories of Dragon Hunters will be essential for other character's HP% and ATK buff.



Barbara Best Weapon

Barbara's best weapon is Extraordinary Stories of Dragon Hunters. This catalyst will buff the ATK of the new character who enters the field after Barbara and also has HP% as an attribute, which further increases the character's healing.

Other Weapon options for Barbara are: Fumetsu Gekka, Favonius Codex and Stories from Other Worlds.



Barbara Best Artifact Set

Sea Dyed Shell is the best artifact set for Barbara Support. This set, in addition to healing, can cause damage to enemies based on the healing done. If you haven't unlocked the Inazuma areas yet, in Mondstadt, you can farm the Beloved Maiden's set.

Barbara Artifacts Sub-Attributes

For the sub-attributes of the artifacts, the priorities are HP%, and Energy Recharge, nothing else is necessary, attributes other than these will not help with Barbara's healing, other than HP Flat.


Barbara Talent Priority

Barbara's Talent that you should prioritize is the Elemental Skill, as it is the character's greatest source of healing as a Support. the Ultimate is second priority and the Basic Attack is not necessary.

Barbara Talents



Normal Attack: Breath of Water


Normal Attack


Performs up to 4 water splash attacks that inflict Hydro Damage.


Charged Attack


Consumes a certain amount of Stamina to inflict AoE Hydro Damage after a short period of channeling.


Immersive Attack


Gathering the power of Hydro, Barbara dives towards the ground in mid-air, inflicting damage on all enemies in her path. Inflicts AoE Hydro Damage upon impact with the ground.


Let the Show Begin♪


Summons water droplets that resemble musical notes that form a Melodic Circle, dealing Hydro Damage to nearby opponents and applying the Wet state to them.


Melodic Circle


• On hit, Barbara's normal attacks heal her own team members and nearby allied characters for a certain amount of Health, which scales with Barbara's Max Health.

• Landing Barbara's Charged Attack generates 4 times the amount of healing.
•Periodically regenerates your own active character's Health.
•Applies the Wet state to the character and opponents who come into contact with them.


Brilliant Miracle♪


Heals all allied forces and your own character for a large amount of Health that increases with Barbara's Max Health.



Glorious Season


The Stamina Consumption of characters within the Let the Show Begin♪ melody loop is reduced by 12%.


Encore


When your own character gains an Elemental Orb/Particle, the duration of the Let the Show Begin♪ Melody Loop is extended by 1s. The maximum extension is 5s.


With All My Heart♪


When Perfect Cooking is done on a food with a recovery effect, there is a 12% chance of receiving double production.
