Cyno Best Build (Main DPS)

The best build for Cyno is Main DPS. This build will allow Cyno to be able to use his Ability more frequently whenever an Electro-related reaction is triggered. With Ultimate activated, Cyno can easily trigger reactions and deal consecutive heavy damage.



Cyno Best Weapon

The best weapon for Cyno is the Crimson Sands Staff. It increases ATK based on Elemental Proficiency making it easier for you to focus on Proficiency. It also increases ATK when hitting an enemy with Elemental Skill and provides Critical Rate as its main stat.



Better

Crimson Sands Staff

Critical Rate

Obtains ATK Bonus based on 52-104% of the Elemental Proficiency of the character who has this weapon equipped. When the Elemental Skill hits an enemy, it generates the "Dreams of Scarlet Sands" effect for 10s: it obtains ATK Bonus based on 28-56% of the Elemental Proficiency of the character who has this weapon equipped. This effect can be stacked up to 3 times.


Optional

Primal Jade Spear

Critical Rate

On hit, increases ATK by 3.2-6.0% for 6s. Maximum 7 stacks. This effect can only occur once every 0.3s. While in possession of as many stacks as possible, Damage dealt is increased by 12-24%.


Other Weapon options for Cyno are: Duel Spear, White Tassel, Dark Cliff Spear and Homa's Staff.



Cyno Best Artifact Set

The best artifact set for Cyno is Raging Thunder. The two pieces will increase the Electro Damage Bonus and the four pieces will increase the damage of some Elemental Reactions and decrease the cooldown of the Elemental Skill.

Cyno Artifacts Sub-Attributes

In the sub-attributes of the artifacts, in order for you to be able to deliver the maximum of Cyno's potential, the priorities are Critical Rate and Critical Damage. Elemental Proficiency, Energy Recharge and ATK% are also great attributes, but they are second priority.


Cyno Talent Priority

The Cyno Talent you should prioritize is Ultimate. The Elemental Skill is second priority and the Basic Attack does not need to be raised or can be left until last.



Cyno Best Teams


It is highly recommended that Cyno always be paired with a support with a shield to protect from interruptions, because he deals a lot of damage quickly with his Normal Attacks during his Ultimate and since his duration is not that long, it would be better if he was not interrupted.

Charged Attack


Consumes a certain amount of Stamina to launch yourself forward, inflicting Damage along the way.


Immersive Attack


Dives mid-air to slam the ground, damaging enemies along the way and dealing AoE DMG on impact.


Secret Ritual: Abyssal Crossing


Advance quickly, inflicting Electro DMG against enemies in your path.

When Cyno is under the "Pathbreaking Oath" state of his Ultimate “ Sacred Ritual: Lupine Agility ”, he will cast Mortuary Ritual, using the power of lightning to inflict AoE Electro DMG, and extend the duration of “Brandbreaking Oath”.


Sacred Ritual: Lupine Agility


Summons a divine spirit to possess you, transforming you into the Pathfinder Oath.



Pathfinder Oath


The Damage that Cyno inflicts from Normal, Charged and Immersive Attacks is converted to Electro Damage which cannot be replaced;
• Increases Cyno's Elemental Proficiency, increases his Resistance against Interruption, and makes him immune to Damage inflicted by the Electrically Charged reaction.
• This effect will disappear when Cyno leaves the battlefield, and lasts up to 18s.



Judgment of the Feathers


When Cyno has the Pathfinder Oath state activated by Sacred Ritual: Lupine Agility, Cyno will periodically enter the "Seer of the End" state. If he casts Secret Ritual: Abyssal Crossing while in this state, it will trigger the "Adjudication" effect, increasing the Damage of this Secret Ritual: Abyssal Crossing by 35%, and firing 3 Desolation Bolts that inflict 50% of Cyno's ATK as Electro Damage.

Desolation Ray Damage is considered Elemental Skill Damage.


Sovereignty of the Nine Bows


Cyno's Damage values ​​increase based on Elemental Proficiency as follows:

• Normal Attack Damage in the Pathfinder state is increased by 125% of Elemental Proficiency.
• The Desolation Ray Damage of your Passive Talent "Feather Judgment" is increased by 250% of Elemental Proficiency.


The Gift of Silence


Earn 25% more rewards when sent on an Expedition in Sumeru for 20 hours.
