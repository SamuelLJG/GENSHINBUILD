Chongyun Best Build (Main DPS)

The best build for Chongyun is Main DPS. This build is focused on improving the damage of Chongyun's Normal Attacks and Charged Attacks, so that he can spend most of his time on the field dealing with most enemies alone.



Chongyun Best Weapon

The best weapon for Chongyun is the Wolf's Grave. This is the best generic sword in the game. It increases the character's entire ATK, consequently also increasing the damage of all of Chongyun's skills and even his Physical Attack. Another weapon that can be replaced by the Wolf's Grave is the Serpent's Backbone.



Better

Wolf's Tomb

ATK %

Increases ATK by 20-40%. When landing the counter attack on opponents with less than 30% Health, increases the ATK of all teammates by 40-80% for 12s. This effect can be activated once every 30s.


Optional

Serpent's Backbone

Critical Rate

When the player is in combat, every 4s increases damage dealt by 6-10% and 3-2% of Damage received. This effect can be stacked up to 5 times, not being reset after the character leaves the field, but will reduce 1 layer of the effect when the character receives Damage.



Chongyun Best Artifact Set

The best artifact set for Chongyun is the Winter Hero. This set of artifacts increases Cryo Damage by 15% and Critical Rate by 20%, and if enemies are affected by Cryo the Critical Rate will also increase by +20%.

Chongyun Talents



Normal Attack: Doom of Demons


Normal Attack


Perform up to 4 consecutive strikes.


Charged Attack


Drains Stamina over time to perform a continuous spinning attack against all nearby enemies. At the end of the sequence, he performs a powerful cut.


Immersive Attack


Dives mid-air to slam the ground, damaging enemies in its path and dealing AoE DMG on impact.


Spiritual Blade - Chongyun's Frost


Chongyun strikes the ground with his Sword, causing a circular AoE Cryo explosion in front of him that inflicts Cryo DMG. After a short delay, the cold air created by the Cryo explosion will coalesce into a Chonghua Frost Field, in which all characters using Sword, Sword, or Spear will be imbued with Cryo.


Spiritual Blade - Shooting Star


Creating the secret artisanal seals, Chongyun summons 3 spiritual blades in mid-air that fall to the earth one by one after a short period of time, exploding as soon as they touch the ground. When spirit blades explode, they inflict AoE Cryo DMG and launch enemies.



Steady Breathing


Characters who use Sword, Sword and Spear within the field created by Spiritual Blade - Chongyun's Frost will have Speed. Normal ATK increased by 8%.


Ice Hunter's Blade


When the area created by the Spirit Blade - Chongyun's Frost dissipates, another spirit blade will be summoned to attack nearby enemies, inflicting 100% Chongyun's Frost Skill Damage as Cryo Damage in an AoE. Enemies struck by this blade will have their Cryo RES decreased by 10% for 8 seconds.
