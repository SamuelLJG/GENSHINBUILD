Dehya Best build (Main DPS)

The best build for Dehya is Main DPS. The objective of this build is to maximize the Ultimate's Damage and make it return more quickly, so that the character can perform its Main Damage function, being able to stay almost all the time on the field.



Dehya Best Weapon

The best weapon for Dehya is the Sign of the Seas. Her passive increases ATK and Maximum Life, which is very good for a character that scales with HP, it also has a Critical Rate bonus, allowing you to have more Critical Damage on artifacts.



Better

Sign of the Seas

Critical Rate

When Elemental Skill hits an enemy, increases ATK by 20-40% for 8s. When taking Damage, increases ATK by 20-40% for 8s. The two effects described above can be triggered even if the character is not active on the battlefield. Additionally, characters who are not under the protection of a Shield will have their Maximum Health increased by 32-64%.


Optional

Serpent's Backbone

Critical Rate

When the player is in combat, every 4s increases the damage dealt by 6~10% and 3~2% of the Damage received. This effect can be stacked up to 5 times, not being reset after the character leaves the field, but will reduce 1 layer of the effect when the character receives Damage.


Other Weapon options for Dehya are: the Luxurious Sea Lord, the Tomb of the Wolf and the Greatsword of Favonius.



Dehya Best Artifact Set

The best set of artifacts for Dehya is the Seal of Insulation, because it increases the damage of the Ultimate, provides Energy Recharge and converts it into damage, which is essential for Dehya who will be on the field most of the time using his Ultimate.

Dehya Artifacts Sub-Attributes

For artifact sub-attributes, the priorities are Critical Rate and Critical Damage. You can also consider the HP% and Elemental Proficiency stats, which can further increase Dehya's overall damage along with the Critical Damage and Critical Rate stats.


Dehya Talent Priority

Dehya's Talent that you should raise first is her Ultimate, as it is Dehya's biggest source of Damage in the Main DPS role, Normal Attack will be second priority and Elemental Skill third.



Dehya Best Teams


For Dehya's teams, it is recommended that she be paired with at least two Hydro characters to obtain resonance that will increase the character's Maximum Life by 25%, which is great for the character, as her General Damage scales with Life.


Dehya Germination Team

Dehya will be the Main Damage of this germination team, Nahida and Kokomi will be able to continuously produce Dendro Cores for Dehya to farm, Yelan will deal Off-Field Hydro Damage and activate Hydro resonance, which is beneficial for her, Dehya and Kokomi.

Dehya Talents



Normal Attack: Golden Sword Technique


Normal Attack


Perform up to 4 consecutive strikes.


Charged Attack


Drains Stamina over time to perform continuous cuts. At the end of the sequence, he performs an even more powerful cut.


Immersive Attack


Dives mid-air to slam the ground, damaging enemies in its path and inflicting AoE damage on impact.


Molten Hell


Depending on the combat situation, Dehya uses different combat techniques.


Flaming Courage


Casts when there is no Dehya-created “Flaming Prison” active on the battlefield.
Deals AoE Pyro DMG and generates a Flame Prison field.


Field of the Incinerating Sword


Casts when there is a Dehya-created Flame Prison active on the battlefield.
Performs a jumping attack, dealing AoE Pyro DMG and again generating a Flame Prison field in a new position.
The newly generated Flame Prison field will inherit the duration of the original field.

You can only cast an Incinerating Sword Field for the duration of a Flame Prison field.


Flaming Prison


When enemies within this field take Damage, Flame Prison will perform a Coordinated Attack based on Dehya's ATK and Max Health, dealing ADE Pyro DMG. This effect can trigger once every 2.5s.
Increases the Resistance against Interruption of active characters within the field, and when these characters suffer Damage, it mitigates part of that Damage, converting it into “Blood of the Red Lioness”, with Dehya suffering that Damage for 10s. When the stored Damage reaches or exceeds a certain proportion of Dehya's Maximum Health, it will stop absorbing it.

It is only possible for there to be one Flame Prison created by Dehya simultaneously.


Lioness Bite


Unleashing her fiery fury and throwing her blade aside, Dehya enters the “Flaming Lioness” state, increasing her Resistance against Interruption. In this state, Dehya will automatically and continuously throw Flame Lioness Punches based on her ATK and Max Health, dealing Pyro DMG. When the duration ends, she will perform a Flame Kick that deals AoE Pyro Damage based on her ATK and Max Health. If the “Flaming Prison” field of Dehya's Elemental Skill “Molten Hell” is active on the battlefield when launched, she will collect it, and when the duration of Flaming Lioness ends, it will generate again in a new position, when generating again, will inherit the duration of the collected field. In this state, Dehya will not be able to cast her Elemental Skill, she will not be able to make Normal, Charged and Immersive Attacks. Her “Normal Attack: Golden Sword Technique” and Elemental Skill “Molten Hell” will be replaced by “Roar Strikes”.


Roar Strikes


Launching a Roar Strike within 0.4s of casting Flame Lioness Punch will increase the casting speed of the next Flame Lioness Punch.



Honest Truth


When Life is below 40%, Dehya will regenerate Life based on 20% of her Maximum Life, and for the next 10s, Dehya will regenerate Life every 2s based on 6% of her Maximum Life. This effect can trigger once every 20s.


Generous Help


In the 6s after Dehya collects Flame Prison, casts Molten Hell – Field of Incinerating Sword or Lione's Bite, the Damage Dehya takes from Red Lione's Blood will be reduced by 60%. This effect can be activated once every 2s.
Additionally, within 9 seconds of casting Molten Hell – Field of Incinerating Sword, she will grant the “Golden Molten Body” status to all team members. When characters under the Golden Molten Body state enter the Incinerating Sword field, they will gain additional Interruption Resistance. It is possible to generate the Golden Molten Body state once every 18s.


Enlightened Journey


During the day (06:00 – 18:00), team characters receive an increase in Speed. Move. of 10%.
This effect does not apply to Domains, Decimated Domains, and Abyss Spire. Furthermore, this does not stack with Passive Talents of equal effect.
