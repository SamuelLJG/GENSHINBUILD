Alhaitham Best Build (Main DPS)

The best build for Alhaitham is Main DPS. This build focuses on maximizing the damage of Alhaitham's Elemental Skill, Ultimate, and Normal Attack. As a Dendro character, Haste and Super Bloom reactions will help you maximize your Overall Damage.



Alhaitham Best Weapon

The best weapon for Alhaitham is the Light of the Cut Leaves. This is Alhaitham's signature weapon. It increases Critical Rate and Damage, and increases the damage dealt by Normal Attacks and Elemental Skill, based on the character's Elemental Proficiency. Another option would be Floral Rain.

Alhaitham Artifacts Sub-Attributes

For artifact sub-attributes, the priorities are Critical Rate and Critical Damage. You can also have Energy Recharge and Elemental Proficiency, but these attributes are second priority. It is recommended that Alhaitham has between 140 to 160% Energy Recharge.


Alhaitham Talent Priority

Alhaitham's Talent that you should level up first is the Elemental Skill, as it scales with Skills and will be what Alhaitham will use most on the field. The Normal Attack is also important, but it is second priority and the Ultimate can be leveled last.



Alhaitham Best Teams


The best teams for Alhaitham are usually those containing Hydro and Electro type characters, so that together with the character's Dendro element, Acceleration and Super Bloom reactions can be triggered. Therefore, you can choose Alhaitham's companions based on these elements.

Cutting Mirror


When cast, Alhaitham will generate 1 Slashing Mirror; if he does not already have any Slashing Mirrors, he will generate an additional one. The Slashing Mirror has the following effects:
• When possessing Slashing Mirrors, Alhaitham's Normal, Charged and Immersive Attacks will be converted into Dendro Damage that cannot be replaced by other elements;
• When the attack described above hits an enemy, the Slashing Mirror will perform the Light Curtain attack, dealing AdE Dendro Damage based on the number of Slashing mirrors;
• It is possible to have up to 3 Cutting Mirrors;
• Slashing Mirrors will disappear over time, and they will all be eliminated when Alhaitham leaves the battlefield.


Alienation: Restriction of Illustration


Creates a special realm, dealing multiple instances of AoE Dendro Damage.
If you have Slashing Mirrors when casting, you will consume all of them to increase the number of times the Damage dealt.
After 2s, if when casting this ability you consumed 0/1/2/3 Slashing Mirror(s), it will generate 3/2/1/0 Slashing Mirror(s) for Alhaitham.



Four Principles of Rectification


When Alhaitham's Charged or Immersive Attacks hit an enemy, they will generate a Slashing Mirror. This effect can trigger once every 12s.


Revelation of the Forest of Enigmas


Each point of Alhaitham's Elemental Proficiency will increase the Damage dealt by Curtain of Light and Alienation: Restriction of Illustration by 0.1%.
You can increase the Damage of Light Curtain and Alienation: Illustration Restriction by up to 100% this way.


Law of Overdetermined Reduction
