Kaeya Best Build (Sub-DPS)

The best build for Kaeya is the Sub-DPS, focused on causing a large amount of Cryo Damage with his Ultimate, helping the active character off the field by causing damage and applying cryo, making room for elemental reactions.



Kaeya Best Weapon

The best one for Kaeya is the Primal Jade Cutter, due to its high Critical Rate, good Base ATK and its great passive. It's simple, but it remains the most effective option for Kaeya. Another option is the Lion's Roar.

Kaeya Artifacts Sub-Attributes

For artifact sub-attributes, the priorities are Critical Rate and Critical Damage. You can also have ATK%, Energy Recharge, and Elemental Proficiency, but these attributes are second priority.


Kaeya Talent Priority

Kaeya's Talent that you should prioritize is Ultimate, as it is the source of all the character's damage as Sub-DPS, and scales with the artifact's passive. Elemental Skill is second priority and Basic Attack is not required.



Kaeya Best Teams


The best teams for Kaeya are PermaFreeze, Melt/Freeze, and Superconduction. These are where Kaeya fits best as an Off-Field Sub-DPS, not needing to stay on the field all the time, only using his Ultimate when necessary.
