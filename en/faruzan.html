Faruzan Talents



Normal Attack: Twist Shot


Normal Attack


Make up to 4 consecutive attacks with the bow.


Charged Attack


Fires a more accurate Aimed Shot with increased Damage.
When aiming, powerful winds will accumulate at the arrowhead. A fully charged wind arrow inflicts Anemo DMG.


Immersive Attack


Fires a hail of arrows into the air before falling and hitting the ground, hitting opponents along the way and dealing one AoE DMG on impact.


Nasamjnin's Kingdom of Wind


Faruzan deploys a polyhedron that deals AoE Anemo DMG to nearby opponents. She will also enter the Gale Manifest State.
While in the Manifest Gale state, Faruzan's next fully charged shot will consume this state and become a Hurricane Arrow that contains high-pressure currents. This arrow deals Anemo Damage based on the DMG of a fully charged "Normal Attack: Twist Shot" shot.


Pressurized Collapse


The Hurricane Arrow will apply a Pressurized Collapse effect to the affected opponent or character. This effect will be removed after a short delay, creating a vortex that deals AoE Anemo DMG and pulls in nearby objects and opponents. If the Hurricane Arrow does not hit any opponents or characters, it will create a Pressurized Collapse effect at its point of impact.
Vortex Damage is considered an Elemental Skill.


Secret Paths of the Wind


Faruzan deploys a Dazzling Polyhedron that releases a Whirlwind Pulse and deals AoE Anemo DMG.
As long as the Dazzling Polyhedron persists, it will continually move along a triangular path. Once he reaches each corner of this triangular path, he will release 1 more whirlwind pulse.


Whirlwind Pulse


• When Whirlwind Pulse is released, it applies the Burden of the Wicked Wind to nearby opponents, decreasing their Anemo RES.
• Whirlwind Pulse will also apply the Merciful Wind Boon to all nearby party members when released, granting them an Anemo Damage Bonus.



Impetuous Current


When Faruzan is in the Gale Manifest state created by Nasamjnin's Realm of Wind, the amount of time required to charge a shot is reduced by 60% and she can apply Wicked Wind Burden from Secret Paths of Wind to opponents who are hit by the vortex created by pressurized collapse.


Lost Wisdom of the Seven Caves


When characters affected by Secret Paths of the Wind Gift of the Merciful Wind deal Anemo Damage using Normal, Charged, Immersive, Elemental Skills, or Elemental Bursts to opponents, they will gain the Hurricane Guard effect : This Damage will be increased based on 32% of Base ATK from Faruzan. 1 instance of Hurricane Guard can occur once every 0.8s. This Damage Bonus will be cleared after the Merciful Wind Boon expires or after the effect is triggered once.


Traces of Knowledge


Earn 25% more rewards when dispatched on a Sumeru Expedition for 20 hours.
