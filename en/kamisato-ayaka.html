Ayaka Best Build (Main DPS)

The best build for Ayaka is Main DPS. The goal of this build is to increase the damage across all of Ayaka's talents, making her a field Cryo DPS that can deal most of the damage alone.



Ayaka Best Weapon

The best weapon for Ayaka is the Reforged Mist Cutter. This weapon greatly increases Ayaka's elemental damage and provides Critical Damage as its main attribute, meaning you won't have much difficulty getting the character with all her stats well balanced.

Ayaka Artifacts Sub-Attributes

In the sub-attributes of the artifacts, in order for you to be able to deliver the maximum of Ayaka's potential, the priority will be Critical Damage. There is no need for Critical Rate because the Winter Hero set will already provide plenty. From second priority you can have ATK and Energy Recharge.


Ayaka Talent Priority

For Ayaka, all her talents are necessary. The talent you should prioritize is Ultimate, as it is the talent that will deal the most damage in the character's kit. Normal Attack is the second one that should be raised and Elemental Skill is the third.



Ayaka Best Teams


Because of the set of artifacts that Ayaka uses in this build, she needs to be paired with Hydro characters, because they will freeze enemies and give the set bonus that will increase the Critical Rate against frozen enemies by 20%.


Ayaka PermaFreeze Team

In this team, Ayaka will act as Main Damage and Shenhe, Kazuha and Kokomi as Supports, Shenhe providing additional ATK and cryo resonance, Kokomi healing and applying hydro freezing enemies that are affected by cryo and Kazuha gathering enemies and reducing their cryo resistance.
