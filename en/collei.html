Collei Best Build (Sub-DPS)

The best build for Collei is Sub-DPS. The objective of this build is to make Collei a Sub-DPS that reduces the dendro resistance of enemies, which can help a lot in reactions that involve Dendro Damage and also cause a good amount of Dendro Damage.



Collei Best Weapon

The best weapon for Collei is the Elegy of the Final Sigh. This bow provides Elemental Proficiency, Energy Recharge, and ATK%, which are all buffs Collei benefits from. The Elemental Proficiency and ATK% buff are also provided to the entire team.



Better

Elegy of the Final Sigh

Energy Recharge

A part of the "Millenary Concert" that floats in the wind. Increases Elemental Proficiency by 60/75/90/105/120 points. When the Elemental Skill or Ultimate hits enemies, the character will obtain a Token of Remembrance, which can be activated once every 0.2s, even when the character is not on the battlefield. Having 4 Symbols of Remembrance consumes them all, and makes all characters in the team obtain the effect of "Millennial Concert: Farewell Music" for 12s: Elemental Proficiency will be increased by 100-200 points, however ATK will be increased by 20 /25/30/35/40%. Within 20s after activation, it is not possible to obtain the Tokens of Remembrance again. Among all effects of multiple values, effects of the same value cannot be accumulated.


Optional

Arch of Favonius

Energy Recharge

CRIT hits have a 60-100% chance of generating a small amount of Elemental Particles, which will regenerate 6 Energy for the character. Can only occur once every 12-6%s.


Other Weapon options for Collei are: Winter Star, Sacrifice Bow and Aqua Simulacra.



Collei Best Artifact Set

The best artifact set for Collei is Memories of the Forest. The two pieces will provide 15% Dendro Damage Bonus and the four pieces will decrease enemie's Dendro RES by 30% if they are hit by Collei's abilities.


If you already have a team member using Memories of the Forest, Golden Dreams can be another good option for Collei, this set will greatly increase her individual damage.

Collei Artifacts Sub-Attributes

For artifact sub-attributes, the priorities are Critical Rate, Critical Damage, and Energy Recharge. It is recommended that Collei has between 180 and 200 Energy Recharge so that she does not have problems with her Ultimate.


Collei Talent Priority

Collei's Talent that you should prioritize is Ultimate, as it is the character's greatest source of damage as a Sub-DPS. Elemental Skill is second priority and Basic Attack is not required.



Collei Best Teams


The best teams for Collei are those involving dendro, electro and hydro reactions, these are: Flowering, SuperFlowering, Acceleration, Intensification and Propagation. In these teams, Collei will be the Sub-DPS dendro applicator, causing a lot of damage and reducing the enemie's dendro resistance.


Collei SuperFlowering Team

Collei will be the Dendro applicator. Xingqiu will react with Dendro triggering bloom reactions. Kuki Shinobu will heal and trigger super bloom reactions. Sucrose will help with crowd control and lower enemie's elemental resistance.

Collei Talents



Normal Attack: Homage to Archery


Normal Attack


Perform up to 4 consecutive shots with a bow.


Charged Attack


Performs a more accurate Aimed Shot with more damage. While aiming, the Dendro element will accumulate at the arrowhead. A fully charged arrow will deal Dendro Damage.


Immersive Attack


Fires a hail of arrows into the air before hitting the ground, dealing AoE DMG on impact.


Caress of Flowers


Launches a Floral Boomerang that deals 1 hit of Dendro Damage to opponents hit.

The Floral Boomerang will return after a while, inflicting Dendro Damage again.


Meowy Secret


The reliable Collambei arrives to save the day!

Throws the doll called Collambei, causing an explosion that inflicts AoE Dendro Damage and creating a Collambei Zone. Collambei will constantly bounce within this zone, inflicting AoE Dendro DMG.



Floral Return


If before the Floral Boomerang returns, one of the team members triggers the elemental reaction of Burn, Acceleration, Intensification, Propagation, Bloom, Super Bloom or Germination, it will grant the character the “Bloom” status, which will continuously inflict Dendro Damage against nearby enemies equivalent to 40% of Collei's ATK for 3s. Triggering a new "Sprout" effect within the duration of the existing “Sprout” effect will nullify the original effect. Damage inflicted by the “Sprout” effect is considered Elemental Skill Damage.


Stillness of the Forest


When a character within the Collambei Zone triggers a Burn, Haste, Intensify, Spread, Bloom, Super Bloom, or Germination elemental reaction, the duration of the area will be extended by 1s.
It is possible to extend the duration up to 3 times this way in one use of Meowing Secret.


Flying Champion of Sumeru


Decreases the Glide Stamina consumption of all your characters by 20%.
Cannot be stacked with Passive Talents that grant exactly the same effects.
