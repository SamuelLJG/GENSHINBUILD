Gorou Best Build (Support)

The best build for Gorou is Support. This build focuses on increasing the support time Gorou provides and energizing the team.



Gorou Best Weapon

The best weapon for Gorou is the Final Breath Elegy. This bow provides Elemental Proficiency, Energy Recharge, and ATK%, which are all buffs that Gorou benefits from. The Elemental Proficiency and ATK% buff are also provided to the entire team.



Better

Elegy of the Final Sigh

Energy Recharge

A part of the "Millenary Concert" that floats in the wind. Increases Elemental Proficiency by 60/75/90/105/120 points. When the Elemental Skill or Ultimate hits enemies, the character will obtain a Token of Remembrance, which can be activated once every 0.2s, even when the character is not on the battlefield. Having 4 Symbols of Remembrance consumes them all, and makes all characters in the team obtain the effect of "Millennial Concert: Farewell Music" for 12s: Elemental Proficiency will be increased by 100-200 points, however ATK will be increased by 20 /25/30/35/40%. Within 20s after activation, it is not possible to obtain the Tokens of Remembrance again. Among all effects of multiple values, effects of the same value cannot be accumulated.


Optional

Arch of Favonius

Energy Recharge

CRIT hits have a 60-100% chance of generating a small amount of Elemental Particles, which will regenerate 6 Energy for the character. May only occur once every 12-6%s.


Other Weapon options for Gorou are: Winter Star, Sacrifice Bow and Aqua Simulacra.



Gorou Best Artifact Set

The best artifact set for the Gorou is the Ancient Royal Ritual. It will increase Gorou's Ultimate Damage by 20% and increase the team's ATK by 20% for 12 seconds after using the Ultimate. Another option is the Exile set.

Deals AoE Geo Damage and raises the General's Banner.


General's standard


Provides the following bonuses based on the number of team members who are Geo Element:
• Having 1 Geo Element team member grants the "Robust" effect: DEF Bonus.
• Having 2 Geo Element team members grants the "Unbreakable" effect: Interruption Resistance, in addition to the effect previously mentioned.
• Having 3 Geo Element team members grants the "Destroyer" effect: grants a Geo Damage Bonus, in addition to the previously mentioned effects.

Only 1 General's Banner that Gorou created can exist on the field at any given time. Characters can only benefit from 1 General's Banner at a time. When team members leave the field, the field's effects last for 2s.

Juuga: For Victory


Demonstrating his worth as a general, Gorou deals AoE Geo DMG and creates a field known as the General's Glory to cheer up his teammates.


General's Glory


This field has the following properties:
• This field has identical effects to the General's Banner created by his Elemental Skill, Inuzaka Concentrated Defense, granting bonuses based on the number of team members whose element is Geo, and will also move along with your character current asset.
• Creates 1 Crystalline Collapse every 1.5s that deals AoE Geo Damage to 1 opponent within your AoE.
• It will pull 1 elemental fragment via the Crystallization Reaction within its area of ​​action to your active character's position every 1.5 s.

If a General's Banner created by Gorou himself currently exists on the field when this ability is used, it will be destroyed.
