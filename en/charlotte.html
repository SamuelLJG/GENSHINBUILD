Charlotte Best Build (Support)

The best build for Charlotte is Support. This build focuses on increasing Charlotte's healing and Ultimate's uptime, increasing Energy Recharge to make this possible. Charlotte wasn't made for combat. You should only focus on the support resources for it.



Charlotte Best Weapon

The best weapon for Charlotte is Favoniu's Codex. Although Charlotte may struggle to activate her passive, especially on single targets and/or without enough Critical Rate, it is still one of her best options. It also improves the team's recharge due to the elemental particles generated.



Better

Codex de Favonius

Energy Recharge

CRIT hits have a 60-100% chance of generating a small amount of Elemental Particles, which will regenerate 6 Energy for the character. Can only occur once every 12-6s.


Optional

Amber Prototype

Energy Recharge

Using Ultimate regenerates 4-6 Energy every 2s for 6s. All party members will regenerate 4-6% Health every 2s.


Other weapon options for Charlotte are: Celestial Atlas, Memory of Dust and Stories from Other Worlds.



Charlotte Best Artifact Set

The best artifact set for Charlotte is the Millelith Firmes. It increases health by 20%, increases the ATK of all team members by 20% and also increases the strength of the Shield by 30%, which is great for the character.

Charlotte Artifacts Sub-Attributes

When you are using Charlotte as a Support, the recommended sub-attribute stats are ATK% and Energy Recharge.


Charlotte Talent Priority

Charlotte's talent that you should level up first is Ultimate, followed by her Elemental Skill and lastly her Normal Attack.
