Candace Best Build (Support)

The best build for Candace is the Support build. This build is focused on making Candace a Buffer Support, increasing her Maximum Life so that her Ultimate's buff is greater and using artifacts that allow other allies to be buffed.



Candace Best Weapon

The best weapon for Candace is the Spear of Favonius. While Candace may struggle to activate her passive, especially on single target and/or without enough Critical Rate, it is still one of her best options. It also improves the team's recharge due to the elemental particles generated.



Better

Spear of Favonius

Energy Recharge

Critical Hits have a 60-100% chance of generating a small amount of Elemental Particle, which will regenerate 6 Energy for the Character. May occur once every 12-6s.


Optional

Homa's Crosier

Critical Damage

Life increases by 20-40%. Additionally, it offers an ATK bonus based on 0.8-1.6% of the user's maximum Health. When the wielder's Health is below 50%, this ATK bonus is increased by an additional 1-1.8% of maximum Health.


Other weapon options for Candace are: Black Tassel, The Hook and Celestial Spine.



Candace Best Artifact Set

The best artifact set for Candace is the Ancient Royal Ritual. It will increase Candace's Ultimate Damage by 20% and increase the team's ATK by 20% for 12 seconds after using the Ultimate. Another option would be the Insulation Seal set.

Sacred Ritual: Guardian Crane


Press


Attacks by advancing with its shield, inflicting Hydro Damage against enemies in front of it.


Press and hold


Raise your shield to block enemy attacks, forming a Shield that absorbs Damage based on Candace's Maximum Life and is 250% effective against Hydro Damage. The Shield lasts until the end of the Elemental Skill.
By holding down for a certain time, Candace will complete the charge, and when she launches the skill, the duration ends or the shield is destroyed, she will perform a jumping attack that inflicts Hydro Damage against enemies in front of her.


Sacred Ritual: Tide of the Wagtails


Candace raises her weapon and invokes a divine blessing that inflicts AoE Hydro Damage based on her Max Health, and continuously applies the "Supplication of the Scarlet Crown" status to her own team characters.


Supplication of the Scarlet Crown


It has the following characteristics:
• When the character's Normal Attack inflicts Elemental Damage against enemies, the Damage inflicted increases;
• When the character becomes active on the battlefield, launches a Flood Wave that inflicts Hydro Damage against nearby enemies. This effect has a limit on the number of times it can be triggered within the duration of this ability;
• Characters under this effect who use Sword, Sword or Spear will obtain Hydro Enchantment.



Feather Protection

Bow from the Sands

Primordial Dawn
