Jean Best Build (Support)

The best build for Jean is Support. The objective of this build is to have Jean as a Support: reduce the elemental resistance of enemies, gradually heal allies and also be able to cause considerable damage with her Elemental Skill and her Ultimate.



Jean Best Weapon

The best weapon for Jean is the Primordial Jade Cutter. This sword increases health by 20-40% and will give bonus ATK based on Max Health, which is great for Jean as her Ultimate scales with ATK. Another option is the Amenoma Kageuta Blade.

Jean Best Artifact Set

The best artifact set for Jean is the Green Shadow one. This set will increase Jean's Anemo Damage and decrease the elemental resistance of enemies that are hit by her. Another artifact option is to use four Ancient Royal Ritual pieces.

Jean Artifacts Sub-Attributes

For the sub-attributes of the artifacts, since Jean's healing scales with ATK, it will be the number one priority when it comes to building her as a Support. You can also have the following sub-attributes: Energy Recharge, Critical Damage and Critical Rate.


Jean Talent Priority

Jean's Talent that you should prioritize is Ultimate, as it is the source of all the character's healing as Support, and scales with the artifact's passive. Elemental Skill is the second priority and Normal Attack is third.
