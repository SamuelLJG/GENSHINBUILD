Fischl Best Build (Sub-DPS)

The best build for Fischl is Sub-DPS. This build is focused on maximizing Fischl's Electro Damage and sustaining Oz's presence on the field for as long as possible. She will be an Off-Field character, who will not need to stay on the field all the time so she can deal a significant amount of damage.



Fischl Best Weapon

The best weapon for Fischl is the Winter Star. The passives of this bow will work very well with Fischl, due to how easy it is for her to activate her passive, even when she is out of the field. Another bow option would be the Last Chord.

Fischl Best Artifact Set

The best artifact set for Fischl is Raging Thunder. The two pieces will increase the Electro Damage Bonus and the four pieces will increase the damage of some Elemental Reactions and decrease the cooldown of the Elemental Skill.

Fischl Talents



Normal Attack: Arrows of Guilt


Normal Attack


Perform up to 5 consecutive shots with a bow.


Charged Attack


Performs a more accurate Aimed Shot with increased Damage. While aiming, dark spirits of darkness lightning will heed your Princes's call and reside at the enchanted tip of her arrow. When fully embodied, the vengeful lightning will inflict immense Electro Damage.


Immersive Attack


Fires a hail of arrows into the air before hitting the ground, inflicting AoE DMG on impact.


Wings of the Night Watch


Evokes Oz. The night raven shaped by darkness and lightning descends to the earth, inflicting Electro DMG at a small AoE.
Throughout the skill's duration, Oz will continually attack nearby enemies with Electrifying Balls.

Press and hold to adjust the location of where Oz will be summoned. Press at any time throughout the ability's duration to recall him to Fischl's side.


Night Illusion


Summons Oz to extend his twin twilight wings and defend Fischl.
It has the following properties throughout the duration of the ability:
• Fischl transforms into the form of Oz, incredibly increasing his Movement Speed.
• Attacks nearby enemies with lightning, inflicting Electro DMG on enemies it comes into contact with. Each enemy can only be hit once.
• When the effect of this ability ends, Oz will remain on the field, then his presence duration will reset.



Star Predator


When Fischl hits Oz with a fully charged Aimed Shot, Oz brings Thundering Retribution, dealing Electro Damage at an AoE equal to 152.7% of the arrow's Damage.


Undone Be Thy Sinful Spell


If your own character triggers an Electro Elemental Reaction when Oz is on the field, the enemy will be inflicted with Thundering Retribution, dealing Electro Damage equal to 80% of Fischl's ATK.


My Secret Garden


When dispatched on an expedition in Mondstadt, the time consumed is reduced by 25%.
