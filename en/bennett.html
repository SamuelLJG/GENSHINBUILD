Bennett Best Build (Support)

The best build for Bennett is Support. This build is focused on improving Bennett's Ultimate, healing as necessary and providing a considerable ATK Bonus. Remember that the Ultimate's ATK Bonus only comes from Basic ATK, not ATK %.



Bennett Best Weapon

The best weapon for Bennett is the Sword of Sacrifice, because of the large amount of Energy Recharge and the passive that when dealing damage against an enemy with Elemental Skill, the skill has a 40-80% chance of resetting the Cooldown time.

Other Weapon options for Bennett are: Celestial Sword, Primordial Jade Cutter, Alley Glow and Wooden Sword (Free To Play).



Bennett Best Artifact Set

The best artifact set for Bennett is the Ancient Royal Ritual. It will increase Bennett's Ultimate Damage by 20% and increase the team's ATK by 20% for 12 seconds after using the Ultimate. Another option is the Exile set.

Bennett Artifacts Sub-Attributes

If your Energy Recharge is below 180%, use Energy Recharge on Sand for Bennett. But if he's using a weapon that solves the energy recharge problem, use triple HP% for his artifacts. For sub-attributes, HP% and Energy Recharge are recommended.


Bennett Talent Priority

Bennett's Talent you should level up first is Ultimate. The character who is within the Supreme's area will receive healing, an ATK buff from the Supreme himself and the passive from the Ancient Royal Ritual Set. The Elemental skill is second priority and Normal Attack is not necessary.



Bennett Best Teams

The entire team is focused on damage without elemental reactions. Xiao as Main DPS will greatly benefit from Bennett 's Ultimate which heals and buffs ATK. Albedo will provide additional Damage, while Zhongli 's shield will provide interrupt resistance and decrease damage taken.
