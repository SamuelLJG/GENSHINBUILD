Eula Best Build (Main DPS)

The best build for Eula is Main DPS. This entire build revolves around increasing Eula's Physical Damage, making her Elemental and Ultimate Skills deal the greatest amount of Physical Damage possible, exerting the character's full potential.



Eula Best Weapon

The best weapon for Eula is Song of the Pines. This sword's main attribute is Physical Damage, which greatly benefits Eula. This weapon also has a passive that increases ATK damage and Normal Attack Speed.



Better

Song of the Pines

Physical Damage Bonus

A part of the Millennial Concert that floats in the wind. Increases ATK by 16-32%. When the Normal Attack or Charged Attack hits enemies, the character will obtain a Symbol of Sighs, which can be activated once every 0.3s. Having 4 Whisper Symbols consumes them all, and makes all characters on the team obtain the effect of "Millennial Concert: Song of the Raising of the Flag" for 12s: Speed. Normal Attack will be increased by 12-24%, however ATK will be increased by 20-40%. Within 20s after activation, it is not possible to obtain Whisper Tokens again. Among all effects of multiple values, effects of the same value cannot be accumulated.


Optional

Serpent's Backbone

Critical Rate

When the player is in combat, every 4s increases damage dealt by 6-10% and 3-2% of Damage received. This effect can be stacked up to 5 times, not being reset after the character leaves the field, but will reduce 1 layer of the effect when the character receives Damage.


Other Weapon options for Eula are: Silver Star Buried in the Snow (F2P), Tomb of the Wolf, Rough Sword, Archaic Prototype (F2P), Celestial Pride, Stonebreaker Red Horns and Luxurious Sea Lord.



Eula Best Artifact Set

The best artifact set for Eula is the Pale Flame. This set, of all, is the best for Physical Damage characters, this includes Eula. Another option, however, less effective, would be two pieces from Pale Flame and two from Bloodstained Knight.

Eula Artifacts Sub-Attributes

For artifact sub-attributes, the priorities are Critical Rate and Critical Damage. If possible, it would also be good to have ATK% and Energy Recharge, but the priorities remain the same.


Eula Talent Priority

A large part of Eula's damage comes from her Ultimate, so it must be leveled first, then Normal Attack and then Elemental Skill. All of Eula's talents are important and none of them should be left unlevelled, they are all important for the character's kit.



Eula Best Teams


The best team for Eula is Superdriving. In this team, superconduction reactions will be triggered when the Cryo and Electro elements combine. This reaction will deal AoE Cryo DMG, reducing the Physical Resistance of enemies hit by 40%.

Hold


Swings the sword to slash forward, dealing AoE Cryo DMG to enemies in front, and consuming all of Eula's Impassive Heart stacks.
If the Impassible Heart's effect is consumed, it will decrease the Physical Resistance and Cryo Resistance of enemies hit. Each Impassive Heart will convert into an Ice Vortex Sword, inflicting Cryo DMG on surrounding enemies.


Frozen Waves Sword


Eula swings her Sword, dealing Cryo DMG to surrounding opponents and creating a Luminous Sword that follows her for a duration of up to 7s.
• While present, the Luminous Sword increases Eula's Interruption Resistance.
• When Eula's Normal Attacks, Elemental Skills, and Ultimates deal Damage to enemies, they will charge the Luminous Sword, which can gain an energy charge every 0.1s.
• When its duration ends, the Luminous Sword will fall and explode, dealing Physical Damage to surrounding enemies.
• This Damage scales based on the number of energy charges the Luminous Sword has accumulated.
• If Eula leaves the field, the Luminous Sword will explode immediately.
