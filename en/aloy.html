Aloy Talents



Normal Attack: Rapid Shots


Normal Attack


Perform up to 4 consecutive shots with the bow.


Charged Attack


Performs a more accurate Aimed Shot attack with more damage.
While aiming, ice will accumulate on the arrow. A fully charged arrow will deal Cryo DMG.


Immersive Attack


Fires a hail of arrows into the air before falling and impacting the ground, dealing AoE Damage on impact.


Icy Nature


Aloy launches a Freeze Bomb in the desired direction that explodes on impact, dealing Cryo DMG. After exploding, the Freeze Bomb will split into many Freeze Bombs that will explode upon contact with opponents or after a short period of time, dealing Cryo DMG. When the Freeze Bomb or its bomblets hit opponents, they will decrease the opponent's ATK and grant 1 stack of Coils to Aloy. Aloy can gain 1 stack of Reels every 0.1s.


Coil


Each stack will increase Aloy's Normal Attack Damage.
When she has 4 stacks, Aloy eliminates all Coil effects and gains Quick Ice, increasing Normal Attack Damage and converting her Normal Attacks to deal Cryo Damage.

While under the Quick Ice state, Aloy cannot gain Coil effects. Coil's effect is deleted 30 seconds after she leaves battle.


Dawn Prophecies


Throws a Battery filled with Cryo towards the target before powering it up with an arrow, dealing AoE Cryo DMG.



Combat Perception


When Aloy receives the Coil 's Frost Nature effect , her ATK is increased by 16%, while the ATK of other surrounding team members is increased by 8%. This effect lasts 10s.


Powerful Attack


When Aloy is under the effect of Quick Ice provided by Frost Nature, her Cryo Damage Bonus increases by 3.5% every 1s. The maximum Cryo Damage Bonus increase gained this way is 35%.


Slowly


When Aloy is on the team, team members who come close to small animals that can produce Poultry Meat, Raw Meat, or Cold Fresh Meat will not be easily frightened.
