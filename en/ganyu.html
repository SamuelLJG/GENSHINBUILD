Ganyu Melhor Build (Main DPS)

The best build for Ganyu is Main DPS. This build focuses on increasing the damage of Ganyu's Charged Attacks, through the bows and artifact sets that will be recommended.



Ganyu Best Weapon

The best weapon for Ganyu is the Path of the Hunter bow. It increases the Damage Bonus of all elements by 12-24% and when hitting an enemy with the Charged Attack you get the Tireless Hunt effect. This effect increases Charged Attack Damage by 160-320% of Ganyu's Elemental Proficiency.


A Free-to-Play arc option would be the Pale Lunar Light Prototype. Charged Attacks on weak points will increase Movement Speed ​​by 10% and ATK by 36-72% for 10s.

Ganyu Best Artifact Set

The best artifact set for Ganyu is the Nostalgic Reminiscence set. This set increases ATK by 18% and Normal Attack by 50% when using the Elemental Skill. If you don't have the Nostalgic Reminescence set, another set that works well in Ganyu is the Itinerant Troupe.

Ganyu Artifacts Sub-Attributes

For artifact sub-attributes, the priorities are Critical Rate and Critical Damage. You can also have ATK%, Energy Recharge, and Elemental Proficiency, but these attributes are second priority.


Ganyu Talent Priority

Ganyu as Main DPS will need her Normal Attack maxed out first, as it is her main source of damage. Ultimate is second priority and Elemental Skill is third.



Ganyu Best Teams


The best team for Ganyu can be formed in different ways, including Freeze and Melt teams. Then, you can choose your best team according to the Hydro and Pyro characters you have available.


Ganyu Freeze Team

In this team, Ganyu will act as Main Damage and Shenhe, Kazuha and Kokomi as Supports, Shenhe providing additional ATK and cryo resonance, Kokomi healing and applying hydro freezing enemies that are affected by cryo and Kazuha gathering enemies and decreasing their cryo resistance.
