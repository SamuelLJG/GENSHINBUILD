Abiogenesis Solar Isotoma


The creation of Solar Radiation deals AoE Geo Damage.


Solar radiation


It has the following properties:

• When enemies within the Solar Radiation area are hit, Solar Radiation will generate Transient Flowers that deal AoE Geo Damage.
• Damage dealt scales according to Albedo's DEF.
• Transient Flowers can only be generated once every 2s.
• When a character is located at the location of Solar Radiation, Solar Radiation will accumulate Geo power, lifting them into a crystallized podium. Only one crystallized podium can exist at a time.
• It is only possible to have one Solar Radiation at a time made by Albedo, and it is considered as a Geo Construction.

Press and hold to designate the skill's location.


Rite of Progeniture Tectonic Tide


Under Albedo's command, Geo crystals emerge and explode, dealing AoE Geo DMG in front of him. If there is Solar Radiation in the field created by Albedo himself, 7 Fatal Flowers will be generated in the Solar Radiation zone, blooming violently and causing AoE Geo Damage. If there is Solar Radiation in the field created by Albedo himself, 7 Fatal Flowers will be generated in the Solar Radiation zone, blooming violently and causing AoE Geo Damage.

The damage caused by the explosion and the Fatal Flower will not generate Transient Flowers.



Calcite Might
