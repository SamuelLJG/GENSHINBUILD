Arataki Itto Talents



Normal Attack: Legend of Combat


Normal Attack


Perform up to 4 consecutive strikes. When his 2nd and 4th attacks hit opponents, Itto will generate 1 and 2 charges of Superlative Super Strength respectively. Maximum 5 charges. Triggering this effect will reset the current duration of any existing stack.

Additionally, even when using the Elemental Skill "Masatsu Zetsugi: Akaushi Blast!" or after running for a short period of time, Itto's Normal Attack combo will not reset.


Charged Attack


By holding down to initiate a Charged Attack, you can use Arataki Kesagiri slashes without using any Stamina. Each slash of Arataki Kesagiri will consume 1 charge of Superlative Super Strength. When the final charge is consumed, Itto will unleash an especially powerful attack. When there are no Superlative Super Strength charges available, Itto will perform a single Saichimonji slash.


Immersive Attack


Dives mid-air to slam the ground, damaging enemies in its path and inflicting AoE damage on impact.


Masatsu Zetsugi: Akaushi Blast!


Launches Ushi, the young akaushi bull and auxiliary member of the Arataki Gang, dealing Geo DMG to opponents! When Ushi hits opponents, he will grant Arataki Itto 1 charge of Superlative Super Strength.

Ushi will remain on the field and provide support in the following ways:

• Draws the attention of surrounding opponents and attracts their attacks.
• Inherits a certain percentage of Health based on Arataki Itto's Max Health.
• When attacked, he will provide Arataki Itto with 1 charge of Superlative Super Strength. 1 charge can be obtained this way once every 2s.
• He will flee when his Life reaches 0 or his duration ends. He will grant Arataki Itto 1 charge of Superlative Super Strength when he leaves the field.


Press and hold


Adjusts the launch angle.

Ushi is considered a Geo Construction and Arataki Itto can only have 1 Ushi that he has placed on the field at a time.


Royal Descent: Behold, Itto the Evil One!


It's time to show them the power of the Arataki Gang! For a time, Itto lets his inner Furious Oni King emerge, using his Oni King Kanabou in battle. This state has the following special properties:

• Normal, Charged and Immersive Attacks will be converted into Geo Damage that cannot be replaced.
• Itto's Normal Attack speed will be increased, and ATK will be increased based on his DEF. The first and third attacks will each grant Arataki Itto 1 charge of Superlative Super Strength when they hit opponents.
• Itto loses 20% Elemental and Physical RES.

The Furious Oni King status will be removed when Itto leaves the field.



Arataki Ichiban


When Arataki Itto uses consecutive Arataki Kesagiri, he obtains the following effects:

• Each slash causes subsequent slashes to have 10% more Speed. of ATK.
• Vel. Maximum ATK is increased by 30%. Increases your resistance to interruption.

These effects will dissipate when he stops executing consecutive cuts.


Bloodline of the Crimson Oni


Arataki Kesagiri's damage is increased by 35% of Arataki Itto's DEF.


Itto the Lumberjack


When a party member uses attacks to obtain wood from a tree, they have a 25% chance of obtaining additional wood.
