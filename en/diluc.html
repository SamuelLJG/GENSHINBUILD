Diluc Best build (Main DPS)

The best build for Diluc is Main DPS. This build is focused on increasing Diluc's overall damage, through the weapon, Tomb of the Wolf, and the artifact set, Crimson Flame Witch. Diluc himself is a fairly easy character to build, requiring few conditions to make him strong.



Diluc Best Weapon

The best weapon for Diluc is the Tomb of the Wolf. This is the best generic sword in the game. It increases the character's entire ATK, consequently also increasing the damage of all of Diluc's skills and even his Physical Attack. Another weapon that can be replaced by the Wolf's Grave is the Archaic Prototype.

Diluc Best Artifact Set

The best artifact set for Diluc is the Crimson Flame Witch. This set is the best for Diluc in terms of increasing his General Damage. the two pieces will provide 15% Pyro Bonus, and the four pieces will increase the damage of all reactions that involve pyro.

Diluc Artifacts Sub-Attributes

For artifact sub-attributes, the priorities are Critical Rate and Critical Damage. You can also consider the ATK% and Elemental Proficiency stats, which will further increase Diluc's overall damage along with the Critical Damage and Critical Rate stats.


Diluc Talent Priority

Diluc's Talent that you should level up first is his Elemental Skill, then his Normal Attack, and lastly his Ultimate. Remember that it is extremely important to keep Diluc's three talents at maximum level, as they all contribute to his overall damage.



Diluc Best Teams


The best teams for Diluc are Vaporization and Fusion. The elements, Hydro and Cryo, work very well with Diluc, due to the reactions triggered when they interact with the pyro element. Therefore, the best synergies for Diluc are the elemental characters, Hydro and Cryo.


Diluc Vaporization Team

In this team, Xingqiu will use his Ultimate so that Diluc can inflict Hydro together with the pyro in his Normal Attacks, triggering several Vaporization reactions. Kazuha will decrease enemie's Pyro RES and Bennett will heal and increase the entire team's ATK.
