Kamisato Art: Reflected Beauty


Kamisato Ayato switches positions and enters the " Torrential Reflection " state. After this exchange, he will leave a Water Illusion in its original location. After its formation, the Water Illusion will explode if opponents are nearby or when its duration ends, inflicting AoE Hydro Damage.


Torrential Reflection


In this state, Kamisato Ayato uses his Aquatic Sword to perform quick attacks, causing the Damage of his Normal Attacks to be converted into Hydro Damage in AdE that cannot be replaced by the enchantment of other elements. In addition, it also has the following properties:
• When the Water Sword's attacks hit an enemy, they will give Ayato the Bright Waves effect, increasing the Damage inflicted by the Water Sword based on Ayato's current Maximum Health. The initial maximum number of Brilliant Wave stacks is 4, and it is possible to obtain a stack through the Aquatic Sword every 0.1s. This effect will disappear when Torrential Reflection ends.
• Increases Kamisato Ayato's Resistance Against Interruption.
• Becomes unable to use Charged or Immersive Attacks. Torrential Reflection

will be eliminated when Ayato leaves the battlefield. Using Kamisato Art: Reflected Beauty again while still in the Torrential Reflection state will end that state.


Kamisato Art: Water Garden


It reveals a pure garden that extinguishes all cacophony.
As long as this space exists, Water Flower Blades will constantly fall and attack enemies in their AoE, inflicting Hydro Damage and increasing the Normal Attack damage of characters within their AoE.



Kamisato Art: Chain Between Mountains


Kamisato Art: Reflected Beauty has the following properties:
• After being cast, Kamisato Ayato obtains 2 stacks of Brilliant Waves;
• After his Water Illusion explodes, Kamisato Ayato will obtain a Bright Waves effect equal to the maximum number of stacks possible.


Kamisato Art: Completion of the Rising Moon


When Kamisato Ayato is in the team but not active on the battlefield, if his Elemental Energy is less than 40 points, he will regenerate 2 Elemental Energy points for himself per second.


Kamisato Art: Daily Kitchen


When a Perfect Food is made, there is an 18% chance of receiving a Strange Food of the same type.
